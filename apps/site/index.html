The transparency layer for the AI era

AI usage should be as understandable as screen time, spending, or energy use.

Carbon Canvas begins with a simple idea: people should be able to see how AI fits into their work and daily lives. Environmental impact is the flagship lens, strengthened by privacy-first tracking and a methodology that shows its limits.

01 · USAGE VISIBILITY

See your AI activity across platforms

Bring sessions, platforms, models, frequency, and trends into one understandable view instead of checking separate provider dashboards.

02 · PRIVACY-FIRST

Understand behavior without collecting conversations

Carbon Canvas is designed around minimal metadata, local processing where possible, and clear controls over what is observed and retained.

03 · ENVIRONMENTAL INTELLIGENCE

Connect digital activity to physical infrastructure

Translate AI usage into directional energy, carbon, and water estimates while keeping uncertainty visible.

04 · PRACTICAL INSIGHTS

Turn visibility into better decisions

Receipts, comparisons, trends, and efficiency guidance help users understand their habits without guilt, fear, or simplistic scoring.

Core promise

Make AI usage visible without exposing private conversations.

The product is built to learn from usage signals rather than the meaning of what you say. Conversation text is not stored or transmitted by Carbon Canvas.

Designed to understand

  • — Which supported AI tool is being used
  • — Session timing, frequency, and duration
  • — The model, when the interface exposes it
  • — Directional activity size and usage trends

Not collected for analytics

  • — The meaning of your prompts
  • — The content of AI replies
  • — Your private files or documents
  • — Anything you type on unrelated sites

What the platform is designed to show

A simple first screen, with deeper detail available when users want to understand the evidence.

  • Cross-platform AI usage overview
  • Platform and model breakdowns
  • Usage history and changing habits
  • Energy, carbon, and water estimate ranges
  • Confidence reasons, assumptions, and sources
  • AI receipts and practical efficiency guidance
Use cases

Students, educators, and researchers

Organizations and design partners
