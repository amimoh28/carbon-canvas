One environmental view across the AI tools you already use.

Carbon Canvas is not differentiated by producing one more carbon number. Its advantage is the combination of cross-platform visibility, privacy by design, transparent methodology, and education that helps people act.

Privacy by architecture

Understanding, not surveillance.

Every AI interaction runs on physical infrastructure. Carbon Canvas makes that relationship visible from usage metadata while deliberately leaving the words themselves alone.

We measure

  • — Which supported AI tool is being used
  • — Session timing and frequency
  • — The model, when the page exposes it
  • — A directional estimate of activity size

We never need

  • — Your prompts
  • — The AI’s replies
  • — Your files or documents
  • — Anything you type elsewhere
