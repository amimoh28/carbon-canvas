The gap

Provider dashboards show adoption. Carbon accounting shows the wider footprint. Carbon Canvas connects AI usage with understandable environmental signals.

The goal is not to replace security systems, governance platforms, provider telemetry, or audit-grade emissions reporting. It is to create a defensible learning and decision-support layer between them.

01 · USAGE

Cross-platform patterns

Explore which supported tools are being used, how often, and how usage changes within an agreed voluntary cohort.

02 · ENVIRONMENT

Directional impact ranges

Translate activity into energy, carbon, and water estimates while keeping uncertainty and infrastructure gaps visible.

03 · ACTION

Recommendations and education

Identify opportunities for more intentional workflows without reducing responsible AI to a simplistic “use less” message.

Important boundary

Carbon Canvas outputs are estimates and educational decision-support. They should not be presented as audited emissions, provider telemetry, employee productivity scoring, or a replacement for privacy and security review.
